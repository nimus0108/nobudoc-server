About Nobu


Nobu is a program built by students, for students. Originally worked on at HackRU2014, Nobu is the ideal site for changing your essays with ease. The creators are students at Bergen County Academies as members of the Academy for Technology and Computer Science. We want to create something that can be used for people just like us, who need some help with an essay but have no where to turn. The inspiration for the name came from its Japanese meaning, "to prolong or stretch."

How to Nobu


Simply upload your text into the provided form. With the option to shorten your essay into a more concise version of the subject material, it takes the work out of consistant re-reads of your work to find the perfect combinations of words. No more do you have to worry about going over your assignment's 500 word limit- Nobu can make sure you keep yourself under. However, if you're feeling like you just haven't written enough, Nobu can expand upon your essay to use words with more characters while keeping the meaning of the original subject material intact. Worried about what NobuDOCS is going to change for you? All insertions are underlined and all deletions are crossed-out in the output. In the future we hope to integrate Google Docs APIs and file upload compatibility to make NobuDOCS the best it can be.
